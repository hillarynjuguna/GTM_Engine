# Ideal Customer Profile — Malaysian F&B SME

## Primary ICP (Tier 1)

| Attribute | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Industry** | Food & Beverage (restaurants, cafés, bakeries, catering, food stalls) |
| **Business Size** | 1–3 outlets |
| **Monthly Revenue** | RM 20,000 – RM 150,000 |
| **Employees** | 2–15 staff |
| **Geography** | Urban/suburban Malaysia (Klang Valley, Penang, JB, Ipoh, Kota Kinabalu) |
| **Business Age** | 1–10 years operating |

---

## Digital Maturity Score

> **Level 1–2 out of 5** (Low Digital Maturity)

| Signal | Description |
|---|---|
| **Current POS** | Manual cash register, basic POS, or none |
| **Invoicing** | Handwritten / Excel / receipt books |
| **Order Channels** | Walk-in + WhatsApp (informal) |
| **CRM** | None — customer data lives in phone contacts |
| **Accounting** | Manual or outsourced to part-time accountant |
| **Social Media** | Facebook/Instagram page (rarely updated) |

---

## Psychographic Profile

| Trait | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Mindset** | Hands-on operator, works IN the business not ON it |
| **Tech Attitude** | Skeptical but willing if it saves time/money |
| **Decision Style** | Word-of-mouth driven, needs peer validation |
| **Biggest Fear** | Government penalties, losing customers, wasting money on tools |
| **Language** | Bahasa Malaysia primary, English secondary |
| **Device** | Android smartphone (primary business tool) |

---

## Buying Behavior

| Phase | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Sees Facebook/Instagram ad or hears from supplier/friend |
| **Research** | Asks in WhatsApp groups, watches short demos |
| **Decision** | Needs to see it work on THEIR menu, THEIR phone |
| **Purchase Trigger** | Compliance deadline OR visible revenue loss |
| **Budget Sensitivity** | High — monthly SaaS must feel cheaper than the problem |
| **Preferred Payment** | Monthly (not annual), FPX or online banking |

---

## Urgency Triggers (Why Now)

| Trigger | Timeline |
|---|---|
| **LHDN e-invoicing mandate** | Phased rollout 2024–2027; businesses >RM25M already live, SMEs incoming |
| **SST/tax audit fear** | Ongoing — messy records = penalties |
| **WhatsApp order volume increasing** | Post-COVID behavioral shift is permanent |
| **Competitor adoption** | "My friend's restaurant already has this" |
| **Seasonal peaks** | Ramadan, CNY, year-end — pain peaks during high volume |

---

## Disqualification Criteria

Do **NOT** pursue if:

- Already using a full-stack POS+CRM (StoreHub, iCHEF, etc.)
- Revenue > RM500K/month (different sales motion needed)
- Business is purely dine-in with no delivery/takeaway
- Owner is not the decision-maker (franchise corporate must approve)
- No WhatsApp usage for business

---

## ICP Summary Statement

> **Our ideal customer is a Malaysian F&B owner with 1–3 outlets, doing RM20K–150K/month, currently managing orders on WhatsApp and invoices manually, who is anxious about upcoming e-invoicing compliance and losing customers to slow replies.**
