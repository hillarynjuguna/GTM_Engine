# Ad Creatives — 6 Variants

## Creative Strategy

Three angles, two variants each:
1. **Compliance-Led** — Fear of penalties, urgency of deadline
2. **Revenue-Led** — Capture more orders, make more money
3. **Simplicity-Led** — Easy setup, no tech skills needed

## Creative Testing Framework

### Week 1–2: Test
- Run all 6 variants simultaneously
- RM50/day per variant = RM300/day total
- Measure: CTR, CPL, reply rate

### Week 3: Optimize
- Kill bottom 3 performers
- Scale top 3 with 2x budget
- Create 2 new variants inspired by winners

### Week 4+: Scale
- Double budget on winner
- Test new audiences
- Add retargeting layer

### Key Metrics Per Creative

| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTR | > 2% | 1–2% | < 1% |
| CPL | < RM 30 | RM 30–50 | > RM 50 |
| Reply Rate | > 50% | 30–50% | < 30% |
